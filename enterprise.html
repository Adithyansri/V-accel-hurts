What is Enterprise Software Development Process?

Enterprise Software Development Process is the complete lifecycle of building and then deploying custom software development services according to the particular requirements of the organization. It comprises the stages-

  1. Scope defining
  2. Requirement analysis
  3. Designing of software
  4. Development phase
  5. Testing
  6. Deployment
  7. Maintenance and support
